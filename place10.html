Power Plant

Exploring Electricity Generation & Energy Production

📍 Introduction

A power plant generates electricity using various energy sources. It is essential for modern life and industrial growth.

🏭 About the Plant

We visited a thermal power plant that generates electricity using coal. The plant supplies power to nearby cities and industries.

⚡ Power Generation Process

  • Coal is burned to heat water
  • Steam rotates turbines
  • Generators produce electricity
  • Power transmitted through grid

🔋 Types of Power Plants

  • Thermal (Coal/Gas)
  • Hydroelectric
  • Solar Power
  • Wind Energy

🛡️ Safety & Environment

  • Emission control systems
  • Safety protocols
  • Protective gear for workers
  • Environmental monitoring

🎯 Objectives of Visit

  • Understand electricity generation
  • Learn about turbines & generators
  • Explore power distribution
  • Know renewable energy

👨‍🎓 Student Experience

Students learned how electricity reaches our homes and the importance of energy conservation.

🧾 Conclusion

The power plant visit was informative and highlighted the importance of electricity in daily life.
